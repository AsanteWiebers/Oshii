The Omakase Berry

Pick Your Berry

For a Changing World, for a Curious Palate

The Omakase Berry® is a rare and exceptional variety, once only found seasonally in the fields of the Japanese Alps. Oishii set out to recreate those idyllic conditions with an indoor vertical farm. By replicating the light rain, cool breeze, and bright sun of a Japanese winter’s day, these berries can now flourish all year long.

In the tradition of Omakase, where diners leave the choosing up to the chef, Oishii found further inspiration. Truly, these strawberries summon a culinary experience in every bite. It’s with great pleasure that we unearth this vibrant delicacy, the Omakase Berry®, for all of you.
